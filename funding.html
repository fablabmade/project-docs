Funding strategy

Make a small balance sheet for your product:

  • Identify how much money you need in order to produce it and deliver a first batch to your customers.

  • Identify how will you find this money, including personal funding, friends and family, pre-orders, crowdfunding, investors, loans, etc.

Repeat the project including time information, so you can find out how much resources you need during each period, for example each quarter in an year.
